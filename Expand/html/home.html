Our Objectives

EXPAND is a talent development program designed to foster the start-up mindset across the organization to drive long-term innovation and growth within Johnson & Johnson

  • Develop a start-up mindset

  • Drive innovation and increase organization and leadership agility

  • Expand horizontal knowledge and understanding end-to-end

Do live up to this great era and grow yourself with this great company.

Will Song

Talent is our most valuable asset. We're really excited to bring to life such new programs like EXPAND to create opportunities for our talent to experience, learn and make a bigger impact via an open innovation ecosystem and Johnson & Johnson's innovation network. I hope it can be a quadruple-win among our talent, our start-up partners, Johnson & Johnson as a company, and most importantly, consumers and patients, who can win ultimately as we accelerate making meaningful, transformational innovations for them.

It's these kinds of exchanges, between two worlds, that can help lift our overall capabilities as a company.

Asgar Rangoonwala

Working for a start-up gives our talents a unique experience. They see everything end to end. They see the good, the bad and the ugly. Meanwhile, they also bring a lot of experience, global thinking, and inputs of working at much broader operations day-to-day to those start-ups. It's these kinds of exchanges, between two worlds, that can help lift our overall capabilities as a company.

Innovation happens when we bring the best minds and the best science together to spark new ideas.

Dan Wang

We recognize that innovation does not happen in any one single company or country. Innovation happens when we bring the best minds and the best science together to spark new ideas. The EXPAND program has connected us with the start-ups and provided us new perspectives about innovation.

Be there to figure out the problem and be there to learn.

Alex Kopitsas
